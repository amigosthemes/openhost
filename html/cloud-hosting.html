Is it possible to upgrade my VPS later on?
Yes you can increase the size of your VPS at any time, however you can not downgrade after your container storage is upgraded.
Can I get IPv6 on my VPS?
Yes we do have IPv6 support, although it is not automated. If you contact us, we can enable it on your VPS.

We offer FREE 4gbps / 8 million PPS of Always-On DDoS protection for all of our services as a standard level of protection.
