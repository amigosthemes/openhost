Our Story

Founded in 2008, our company was carefully built to create high-value hosting services and true long-term relationships with our customers and supporters. The values we own today – long-term connections, continuous improvement, being who you are – were rooted by our customers over the years of successful collaboration.
Our top-tier, ISO certified data centers across the globe ensure access to the major business areas in Europe, Americas, Asia, Australia and Africa. Our team strives to ensure immaculate network stability, solid server reliability and excellent customer oriented service.
